About Me

Hello! I'm Sean, an astrophysics PhD student at Queen's University Belfast. I lead the data analysis for the Planet Hunters NGTS citizen science project which you can read more about here.

Education:

Other interests: When I'm not doing science, I'll probably be found riding a bike or watching any sport I can find. I'm an avid watcher of hockey, rugby and Formula 1. I'm also big fan of sitcoms (this website was created while re-watching Community for the nth time).

Research

Planet Hunters NGTS
During my PhD, I am leading the data analysis of the Planet Hunters NGTS citizen science project.

NGTS Scintillation Monitoring
For my MSc by Research, I investigated the noise properties of scintillation-dominated light curves from the NGTS telescopes at the Paranal Observatory in Chile. A gif of the monthly changes in wind speed above South America can be viewed here.
